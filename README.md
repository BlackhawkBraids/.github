## About Blackhawk Braids

**Authentic 550 Paracord Gear • Crafted in the USA • U.S. Shipping Only**

Blackhawk Braids specializes in premium paracord products made with **authentic 550 paracord** that meets military specifications (MIL-C-5040 Type III). Every product is crafted in the USA with precision and pride.

### Why Authentic 550 Paracord Matters

All our products use **genuine 550 paracord** featuring:
- 7 inner strands with 3 yarns each
- Minimum 550 lb tensile strength
- Military-spec nylon construction
- Superior durability and weather resistance

**We never compromise on quality.** Every piece is inspected to ensure authenticity and craftsmanship.
